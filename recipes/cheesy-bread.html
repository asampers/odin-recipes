Stef's Super Cheesy Garlic Bread

Bread, Cheese, and Butter!

Naples34102 says: "Wouldn't have this often as it's heavy and rich and clearly not the best nutrition-wise either. But as an occasional splurge in small portions this was a mouth-watering, gooey, cheesy treat to accompany our main dish salad tonight."

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ¾ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 ¼ cups Parmesan cheese
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, halved lengthwise

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Combine the butter, mayonnaise, green onions, garlic, Parmesan cheese, and Monterey Jack cheese in a large bowl. Cut each half of French bread into 4 pieces. Spread the cheese mixture evenly on the bread pieces.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 8 minutes. Set the oven to broil; broil until hot and bubbly, about 2 additional minutes.

  4. Serve and enjoy!

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 555 calories; protein 17.5g; carbohydrates 35.4g; fat 39g; cholesterol 68.2mg; sodium 872.3mg.

*Not part of a healthy balanced diet. Proceed with caution.
